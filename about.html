    • About us

    Ammon & Rizos is your premier partner, offering unmatched territory sales and marketing expertise for top-tier manufacturers within the electronics industry. As a distinguished manufacturer's representative firm, we extend our reach across the United States, Mexico, and South America, serving as a beacon of excellence in design for Passive and Semiconductor devices. With a robust presence spanning nine strategically located offices, including branches in Brazil and Mexico, and a dedicated team of over 45 professionals, we are uniquely poised to penetrate and excel in the dynamic, high-growth markets of North and South America.

    Our Journey:

    From our very inception, we've taken pride in cultivating a highly technical sales force, distinguishing us as the trusted ally of top-tier global companies. Notably, over 70% of our external sales team possesses technical degrees, with an impressive eight team members holding engineering degrees.
    With an average of 14 years' experience in electronic sales or engineering, our sales force embodies a wealth of knowledge and expertise. This deep reservoir of talent ensures that our team consistently delivers exceptional value to our esteemed customers and Principals. Our dedication to remaining at the vanguard of technological progress is what truly sets us apart. To maintain this leading edge, we devote countless hours to rigorous training, empowering our team to keep stride with the ever-shifting landscape of technology.
